## Getting Started

### Registration and Login

1. **Open the App**: Launch the Kirana app on your mobile device
2. **Enter Phone Number**: On the welcome screen, enter your 10-digit mobile number
3. **Receive OTP**: You'll receive a 4-digit verification code via SMS
4. **Enter OTP**: Type the 4-digit code in the verification screen
5. **Complete Registration**: If you're a new user, provide your name and default delivery address

**Note**: You can request a new OTP if you don't receive it within 60 seconds. There's a limit of 3 OTP requests per hour for security.

### First Time Setup

After successful login:
- Add your delivery address(es)
- Browse the product catalog
- Start shopping!

## Managing Your Cart

1. **Cart Icon**: Tap the cart icon in the top-right corner
2. **Badge Count**: Shows number of items in your cart

**Change Quantity**:

## FAQs

### Account & Login

**Q: I didn't receive the OTP code. What should I do?**
A: Wait 60 seconds and tap "Resend OTP". Ensure your phone number is correct and you have network connectivity.

**Q: How many times can I request OTP?**
A: You can request OTP 3 times per hour. After that, you'll need to wait before requesting again.

**Q: Can I change my phone number?**
A: Currently, phone numbers cannot be changed. Contact support if you need assistance.

### Shopping & Orders

**Q: How do I know if a product is in stock?**
A: Stock status is shown on each product card. "In Stock" means available, "Low Stock" means limited quantity, "Out of Stock" means unavailable.

**Q: Can I modify my order after placing it?**
A: No, orders cannot be modified. You can cancel and place a new order if the status is Pending or Confirmed.

**Q: What payment methods are available?**
A: Currently, only Cash on Delivery (COD) is available. Pay when you receive your order.

**Q: Is there a delivery fee?**
A: Delivery is currently FREE for all orders.

**Q: How long does delivery take?**
A: Delivery times vary based on your location and order volume. You'll receive status updates via notifications.

### Cart & Checkout

**Q: My cart is empty after I closed the app. Why?**
A: Your cart should persist across sessions. If it's empty, there may have been a sync issue. Try adding items again.

**Q: Can I save items for later?**
A: Currently, there's no "save for later" feature. Items remain in your cart until you remove them or place an order.

**Q: What happens if an item goes out of stock while it's in my cart?**
A: You'll be notified during checkout if any items are out of stock. You can remove them and proceed with remaining items.

### Addresses

**Q: How many addresses can I save?**
A: You can save multiple addresses for different delivery locations.

**Q: Can I delete an address?**
A: Yes, unless it's associated with an existing order. Addresses used in orders are preserved for record-keeping.

**Q: What is a default address?**
A: The default address is pre-selected during checkout for convenience. You can change it anytime.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The app is slow or not loading. What should I do?**
A: Check your internet connection. Close and reopen the app. If the issue persists, try clearing app cache or reinstalling.

**Q: I see an error message. What does it mean?**
A: Error messages explain what went wrong. Common issues include network problems or stock unavailability. Try again or contact support.

**Q: Can I use the app offline?**
A: Some features work offline (viewing cart), but you need internet to browse products and place orders.
